About me

Welcome to my academic website! I am a Ph.D. student in Sociology at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am supervised by Prof. TAM Tony Hong wing. My recent research focus on social trust, social perception, and domain-specific AI.

Research Interests

  • Data-driven Scientific discovery;

  • Using Computational Approaches to learn the concepts within Sociology and Social Psychology;

  • Social, Cultural, and Behavioral Modeling;

  • Study Social Trust by combining RELATIONAL and STRUCTURAL Perspectives.
